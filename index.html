Lawrence Bishnoi: Find out everything about the gangster who threatened Salman Khan and allegedly killed Sidhu Moose Wala.

Lawrence is currently locked up in Delhi’s Tihar jail. He who is one of the alleged accused in the murder of Punjabi singer Sidhu Moose Wala is facing several
charges against him. In a now-exclusive interview with ABP from inside the jail, Lawrence Bishnoi has issued an open threat to actor Salman Khan.The gangster expressed, "There is anger in our society for Salman Khan. He humiliated my society. A case was filed against him but he did not apologise. If he does not tender an apology, be ready to face the consequences. I will not depend on anyone else.”"There is anger in my mind for him since childhood. Will break his ego sooner or later. He should come to our deity's temple (Jambeshwarji temple) and apologise. If our society forgives, then I will not say anything," Lawrence added. Reportedly the Bishnoi community has been worshipping blackbuck since their traditional history.Recalling the 1988 incident, Salman hunted a blackbuck during the shoots of his film 'Hum Saath Saath Hain'. Blackbuck is a protected species under the Wildlife Protection Act, of 1972. The actor was even sentenced to five years in jail in a case filed by the Bishnoi community and heard by a Jodhpur court. However, he was granted bail soon after and major charges against him were dropped in 2022.On the work front, Salman last month concluded hosting his TV show Bigg Boss 16 and was seen making a cameo in SRK starrer Pathaan. His last film was 2018’s Antim on the big screen with an interesting lineup arriving. Khan will be seen in Kisi Ka Bhai Kisi Ki Jaan and Tiger 3 this year.The action was taken in Bikaner, Churu, Ganganagar, and Hanumangarh districts of the Bikaner range, as well as in the Udaipur, Jodhpur, and Kota districts. More than 2,100 policemen raided over 350 places simultaneously in these districts. Shubhdeep Singh Sidhu (11 June 1993 – 29 May 2022), known professionally as Sidhu Moose Wala, was an Indian rapper, singer and songwriter. Cause of death: Gunshot wounds.

Sidhu Moose Wala is a Punjabi Singer, Lyricist, Rapper, and Actor associated with Punjabi Music Industry. He is best known for his songs like So High, Same Beef, Tochan, Bambiha Bole, 295, Just Listen, and more. His some of songs have top charted on some International Billboards like the Canadian Albums chart, UK Singles Chart, UK Asian chart, Global YouTube music chart, Canadian Hot 100, and New Zealand Hot charts. Sidhu also made his debut in Punjabi cinema in the film ”Yes I Am Student”, ”Moosa Jatt”, and ”Jattan Da Munda Gaun Lagya”. He has released three studio albums PBX1, Snitches Get Stitches, Mossetape, and one Extended Play ”No Name”. Sidhu is generally regarded to have been one of the greatest Punjabi artists of his generation.
